Product Overview

The Linear Actuator 3000 series is engineered for durability and high performance in demanding industrial environments. It provides precise, repeatable linear motion control, making it ideal for automation, robotics, and manufacturing applications. The IP65-rated enclosure ensures protection against dust and water ingress, allowing for operation in harsh conditions.

Key Features

  • High-efficiency DC motor for low power consumption.
  • Stroke lengths available from 50mm to 1000mm.
  • Dynamic load capacity up to 3000N.
  • Integrated limit switches for end-of-stroke protection.
  • Corrosion-resistant aluminum alloy housing.
